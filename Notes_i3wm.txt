== Config

Files -- Configuration is don in `~/.config/i3/config`.

There are sample `config` files at:

- https://gist.github.com/Insolita/64d8fc80a2c8b6e114e05e07e97928c7

- https://gist.github.com/bcremer/7524492

The cache of the menu items (`dmenu`) is in `~/.cache/dmenu_run`.
